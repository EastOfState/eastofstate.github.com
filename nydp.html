New York Dawg Pound

After losing his job, Will Brown had an epiphany when his friend asked him, "do you want to work again for somebody that can change your life in ten seconds or less?" He realized quickly that the answer was "no." Soon after, he and his co-founder made plans to open New York Dawg Pound near Shawnee Mission Pkwy & Sante Fe Dr.

This one of a kind restaurant focuses on the hot dog. Mr. Brown serves the usual favorites along with his own creations. The atmosphere is casual, with menu items named after dog breeds. Astonishingly both vegetarian and dessert hot dogs are available! A hard-working owner himself, Mr. Brown is there to greet you at the door every day of the week.
