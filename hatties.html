Hattie's Fine Coffee

Among the suburban familiarity of Prairie Village lies a cozy oasis. Hattie’s Fine Coffee offers the smoothest caffeinated boost in town. This homey establishment, located on 83rd and Mission Rd., is a can’t-miss cafe in the newly-refinished Corinth Square.

The cafe was founded in the late nineties by Heather and Katie, hence the name Hattie’s. Ownership changed hands several times before David and Gretchen Copeland purchased the place in April 2010. Gretchen’s history with Hattie’s goes back to college, when she began working there as a barista. Today Gretchen takes interest in crafting better drinks and perfecting latté art. Recent improvements to Corinth Square have attracted business to Hattie’s.

Being a family owned establishment, David and Gretchen split responsibilities on roasting coffee beans and baking goods respectively. Green coffee beans are imported from many regions of the world including Central and South America, Africa and Indonesia. David then uses a speciality coffee roaster that stands prominently next to the bar. The roasting process differs depending on the place of origin. Lighter roasts have more vibrancy and caffeine, while dark roasts give earthy and smoky tones with less caffeine.

Homemade baked goods are prepared by Gretchen herself, which include all of the usual favorites. Hattie’s offers breakfast and brunch menus with gluten-free options as well as fresh fruit smoothies.

Hattie’s satisfies more than just our craving for a cup of coffee, it satisfies our craving for an intimate coffee shop experience in Johnson County.
